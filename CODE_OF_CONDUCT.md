# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in Personal Ledger Pro welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while being clear about risks and tradeoffs.
- Use inclusive language.
- Keep issue and pull request discussion focused on the project.
- Be careful with private financial information and personal data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing another person's private information.
- Posting real financial records, private databases, credentials, or sensitive screenshots.
- Repeated off-topic comments or disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct. Serious or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to the maintainers privately. Include relevant links, screenshots, or context where safe to do so. Do not include private financial data.
